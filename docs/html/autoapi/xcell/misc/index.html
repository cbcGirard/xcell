xcell.misc.current_source_power(current, radius, sigma)#

Get an analytic estimate of the power dissipated by a spherical current source.

Parameters:

  • current (float) – Amplitude of source in amps.

  • radius (float) – Radius of source in meters.

  • sigma (float or float[:]) – Conductivity of surroundings in S/m.

Returns:

power – Estimated power dissipation in watts.

Return type:

float

xcell.misc.estimate_power(voltages, edges, conductances)#

Estimate power dissipated in the equivalent netowrk of the mesh.

Parameters:

  • voltages (float[:]) – Voltage at each node.

  • edges (int[:,2]) – i-th lists indices of the endpoints for the i-th conductance.

  • conductances (float[:]) – Value of conductances in S.

Returns:

Total power dissipated in watts.

float
